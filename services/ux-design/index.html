UX Design for Pharma & Regulated Industries

I design the tools people actually use. Complex systems in pharma, healthcare, and regulated industries - grounded in real workflows, not assumptions. 17+ years of experience including 5 years leading UX at Roche.

Expertise

What I deliver

UX Audits & Heuristic Evaluation

Structured reviews of usability, accessibility, and design consistency. I find the friction points, prioritize what to fix, and give you a clear action plan - not a 200-page report that sits on a shelf.

Design Systems & Atomic UI

Reusable component libraries that keep your product consistent as teams and features grow. Built in Figma with Atomic Design principles and clear documentation that bridges design and engineering.

User Research & Stakeholder Workshops

From 1-on-1 interviews to workshops with 50+ stakeholders. I align diverse teams around shared goals, resolve blockers, and turn conflicting requirements into actionable design direction.

Wireframing & Prototyping

From low-fidelity sketches to interactive Figma prototypes. I validate ideas early, test with real users, and iterate fast - before a single line of code is written.

GxP-Compliant Interface Design

Designing for regulated environments where compliance isn't optional. Audit trails, data integrity, validation requirements - baked into every interaction pattern from day one.

Visual Design & Brand Strategy

Core Expertise products deserve good aesthetics too. I create visual identities and brand guidelines that work across complex product ecosystems while maintaining regulatory clarity.

Process

How I work

Every engagement follows a structured approach - adapted to scale, never skipped.

01

Discover

Stakeholder interviews, user research, and heuristic review of existing systems. I map the real workflows - not the ones in the spec document.

02

Define

Prioritized findings, user journey maps, and a clear design strategy. Everyone agrees on the problem before we start solving it.

03

Design

Wireframes, prototypes, and design system components in Figma. Iterative validation with users and stakeholders at every stage.

04

Deliver

Developer handoff with detailed specs, component documentation, and ongoing support during implementation. The job isn't done at the Figma file.
