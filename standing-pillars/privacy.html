Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 March 2026

1. Who is responsible

Igor Pilicic, operating as Standing Pillars

2. What data is collected

This website collects minimal data:

  • Contact form submissions: Your name, email address, and message text - only when you voluntarily submit the contact form.
  • Automatic language detection: On your first visit, your approximate location is determined via your IP address using the ipapi.co service to suggest the appropriate language version (English, German, or Croatian). Your IP address is sent to this third-party service. No personally identifiable information is stored by this website from this process.
  • Analytics (with consent): If you accept analytics in the consent popup, anonymised usage data (pages visited, time on site, device type) is collected via Google Analytics to improve the site. This only activates after your explicit consent.

3. What is NOT collected

  • No cookies are set by this website
  • No analytics or tracking scripts are used
  • No advertising networks are present
  • No user accounts or passwords are stored
  • No payment information is processed

4. How your data is used

  • Contact form data is used solely to respond to your inquiry. It is not shared with third parties, used for marketing, or added to any mailing list.
  • Language preference is stored in your browser's local storage so the site remembers your language choice. This stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

5. Third-party services

  • ipapi.co - IP geolocation for language detection. Their privacy policy: ipapi.co/privacy
  • Google Fonts - Web fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google may log your IP address. Their privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics - Only loaded if you accept in the consent popup. Collects anonymised usage data. Their privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data storage and retention

Contact form submissions are received via email and retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. No database of contact submissions is maintained.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data held about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact: igor.pilicic@standingpillars.com

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
